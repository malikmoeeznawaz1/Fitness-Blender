Dietary Type: Vegan

Prep Time: 3 min

Cook Time: 0 min

Servings: 1





Citrus, Strawberry, and Cucumber Side Salad



The Dish


We can't get enough of this beautiful side salad. The combination of strawberries, cucumbers, and fresh herbs are delightfully refreshing and flavorful. We often pair this salad with creamy soups, meaty stews, or with roasted proteins like pork, beef, chicken, and turkey. Heavier, more savory dishes that need a little levity for overall balance. This citrus strawberry and cucumber salad is also a pretty well-rounded side. It adapts well to joining most main meals for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.


Hacking this Recipe



Aged balsamic will have a much thicker consistency than a younger balsamic. Even though all balsamic is aged, a younger balsamic comes from a more recent pressing, and will have much more acidity than one aged for longer. A proper aged bottle of balsamic will call out it’s aging and, because of the care taken with a lengthier process, will be a little more expensive. Aged balsamic will drizzle nicely over salads, coating the ingredients, and imparting much more flavor. You may find orange segments already prepared, fresh, in the produce department at your local grocery store. If not, you can use canned mandarin orange slices, if you’re looking at saving some prep time. Just try to avoid mandarin orange slices that are canned in syrup.



ingredients


½ cup strawberry chopped
½ cup cucumber chopped
¼ cup orange segment
1 Tbsp. mint chopped
1 Tbsp.p basil chopped
1 Tbsp.p balsamic
1 Tbsp.p olive oil
